About

The purpose of Redding Soccer camp is to further enrich the soccer community in the surrounding areas.

Coaching Staff

Francisco Basurto is in his fifth year of coaching collegiate soccer and in his 12th year of coaching youth soccer. Basurto has shown consistent success in each of the programs he has been in charge. He currently holds his USSF D License.
